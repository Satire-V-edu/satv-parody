COMP

Between its nine boards — Limbo, Lust, Gluttony, Greed, Anger, Heresy, Violence, Fraud, and Cliched Synecdoche — The Devil’s Advocate provides an impregnable treehouse, surrounded by a moat, bewitched by an ice sorcerer thrice angered only to the most sophisticated writers, artists, critics, designers, coders, dealmakers, butchers, bakers, and candlestickmakers with an interest in the arts, letters, and Claire Messud. Upon completing our semesterly silent pantomiming of Infinite Jest, we select new members through a “Comp” process open to all Harvard undergraduates.

Our Introductory Comp Meeting will be held from 6:23 - 6:15 PM on the fourth Blood Moon of September.

Compers are evaluated exclusively on their pronunciation of Farrar, Straus and Giroux, their mastery of Jack Kerouac’s On the Road, and being Banksy. Use of Buddhist prayer bowls will be graded on a scale of 1 to 53. Please specify all questions either by shouting them from the rooftops (if you are worthy, you will know which ones), or through interpretive whimper. Updates to the Introductory Comp Meetings will be posted to the editor’s Tumblr feed from 2006.
