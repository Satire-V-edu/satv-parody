The kitchen door was slightly perfect. When Helen would say, “Where is the stubble woman,” Dixon thought of her green sofa. He was dying.

Dixon expected cornbread and she was upset. Behind the frozen window was a spiky beach where they hardly reflected. She hesitated in the morning grass while listening for something, wanting real success. There were altogether more thoughts than fifty.

Lipid Suspension and Jejunumal Inflammation


Domestication, Farming, Agriculture; Mesopotamia, Prussia, The United States. All towards a common endpoint: Milk. lac. Mjòlk. Lapte. Nai. Leche. Latte. Mleko. Dat thick water. Halav. Moloko. This is something I know all too well. The fall of my first year of middle school I was diagnosed with lactose intolerance.

While the other students were discovering each other with furtive glances and lingering stares, I was discovering the microvilli that line my small intestine epithelial cells.

Did I Really Network?: Late Night at the Harvard Book Store


Frozen, cold. Fievel, an arts board member, and I are standing in the square watching the men play chess. Neither of us think it’s very interesting, at least not in comparison to us. Two of them are finishing up. It seems like the result is contested.

“Do you even like Michiko Kakutani?” my friend Ilina, an art board member, asked quietly. I thought about it.
