December Cocktail Menu

Dionysus



PEGASUS PUB & COCKTAIL BAR

21 South St

Cambridge, MA 02138


~Short drinks~


The New Yorker-Bound Demigod


1 oz Grey Goose

1 oz dry vermouth

1 oz dry wit

gold leaf-covered cherry on top


The Intra-Advocate Love Affair


1 oz bitters

Shake over ice. Let settle.


~Long drinks~


The Brood


Rinse an old-fashioned glass with red wine. Light a cigarette. Place the rinsed glass upside-down over it on the

bar to trap the smoke. Take a drill and drill a small hole in the side of the glass and insert a classic red-and-white

straw. Sip.


The Executive Board Meeting


Pour St. Germain over a generous quantity of ice. Take a pocket-sized ice pick and thoroughly break the ice.


Old Fashioned Art Bro


Turn a tallboy upside down into an empty pack of Parliaments. Look genuine and make eye contact while you talk

about how disclaiming his faults didn’t absolve DFW of anything and that you therefore feel guilty for liking his

work but so long as you force yourself to remain cognizant of that guilt it’s alright. Adjust your Warbies.


The State of the Advocate Headquarters


Pick up one of the glasses used to trap a cockroach off the floor. Make sure to discard the cockroach. Fill it with

ice chipped off the invading mass in the broken freezer.

Boil the insulation in the attic with 1 cup water and 1 cup sugar to make a simple syrup.

Combine with whatever has fermented in the take-out box that’s been sitting in the stairwell for a month.


~Very long drinks~


The Features Board


1 part babies

Top with anaphora, and then more anaphora, and then more anaphora, and then more anaphora, and then more

anaphora. Don’t kill any of your babies.


The Intra-Advocate Love Affair Variation


Let the bitters settle. Add 1 oz. Sweet Vermouth. Stir it up again.
